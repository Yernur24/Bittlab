KHABIB NURMAGOMEDOV SENDS A MESSAGE TO CONOR MCGREGOR FANS FOLLOWING UFC 229 WEIGH IN SCUFFLE
After things settled down on stage, UFC commentator Joe Rogan interviewed both men about tomorrow’s highly anticipated UFC 229 headliner.
Khabib Nurmagomedov got in the last word and with that delivered one final message to Conor McGregor’s fans and the Irish faithful.
“Tomorrow night, I’m going to smash your boy guys. I’m going to smash your boy. And I want to say thank you all Irish fans, all of the fans around the world. Because of you guys this fight is happening. Thank you guys and tomorrow night, inshallah, and still!”
Khabib Nurmagomedov has gone a perfect 26-0 in his mixed martial arts career, which includes ten victories under the UFC banner.
